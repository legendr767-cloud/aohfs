Our Mission

Transforming Lives Through Education

Education opens the door to opportunity and breaks the cycle of poverty. AOHF runs education centers providing free education, meals, and support, ensuring each child has a chance at a brighter and more secure future through learning.

We believe that every child, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to quality education. Our programs focus on orphaned children, refugees, and vulnerable communities across Africa, providing not just education but also the support systems necessary for children to thrive.

Our Education Programs

Comprehensive educational support designed to meet the diverse needs of vulnerable children

Primary Education

Foundational education for children aged 6-12, covering literacy, numeracy, and basic life skills. We provide free tuition, books, uniforms, and daily meals.

Free tuition and materials
Daily nutritious meals
Qualified teachers

Secondary Education

Advanced learning opportunities for teenagers, preparing them for higher education or vocational training with focus on STEM subjects and life skills.

STEM-focused curriculum
Career guidance
University preparation

Vocational Training

Practical skills training for young adults in trades like carpentry, tailoring, computer skills, and entrepreneurship to ensure sustainable livelihoods.

Hands-on training
Job placement support
Business mentorship

Digital Literacy

Computer and internet skills training to prepare students for the digital age, including basic programming, digital communication, and online safety.

Computer basics
Internet safety
Programming introduction

Psychosocial Support

Counseling and emotional support for children who have experienced trauma, helping them heal and build resilience for their educational journey.

Individual counseling
Group therapy sessions
Trauma-informed care

Adult Literacy

Basic literacy and numeracy programs for adults who missed formal education, empowering parents and community members to support their children's learning.

Flexible schedules
Community-based learning
Practical applications
