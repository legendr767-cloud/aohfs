Our Journey of Impact

Key milestones in our mission to transform lives

2020

Foundation Established

AOHF was founded with a mission to serve vulnerable communities across Africa. Our first program supported 50 orphaned children in Lagos.

50 Children Supported
2021

First Water Project

Completed our first clean water project, providing safe drinking water to 2,000 people in rural Ogun State. This marked our expansion into water and sanitation.

2,000 People Served 5 Wells Built
2022

Healthcare Expansion

Launched mobile health clinics and established our first permanent health center, providing medical care to underserved communities.

10,000 Patients Treated 3 Health Centers
2023

Education Milestone

Celebrated our 100th scholarship recipient graduating from university. Expanded education programs to include vocational training and adult literacy.

100 Graduates 500 Students Supported
2024

25,000 Lives Transformed

Reached the milestone of transforming 25,000 lives across our programs. Expanded operations to 5 African countries with sustainable community partnerships.

25,000 Lives Transformed 5 Countries
