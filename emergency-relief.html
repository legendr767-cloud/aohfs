When Crisis Strikes, We Act Fast

AOHF responds quickly to global crises, from floods and earthquakes to conflicts and displacement. We deliver emergency aid including food, shelter, medicine, and clean water to communities in distress. Whether the crisis is natural or man-made, our team acts fast to ensure help reaches those who need it most.

Our emergency response system is built on preparedness, rapid deployment, and coordinated action with local partners and international organizations to maximize impact and save lives.

Our Emergency Response Process

How we respond to emergencies with speed, efficiency, and compassion

Alert & Assessment

0-2 Hours

Crisis monitoring systems detect emergency. Rapid assessment team deployed to evaluate situation and needs.

Response Planning

2-6 Hours

Emergency response plan activated. Resources mobilized and logistics coordinated with local partners.

Rapid Deployment

6-24 Hours

Emergency teams and supplies deployed to affected areas. Immediate life-saving aid begins.

Relief Operations

1-30 Days

Full-scale relief operations providing shelter, food, medical care, and essential services.

Recovery & Rebuilding

1-12 Months

Long-term recovery support including infrastructure rebuilding and community resilience programs.
