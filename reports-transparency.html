Our Completed Projects

350+ Water Wells Built

Successfully constructed over 350 water wells across rural communities in Nigeria, providing clean drinking water to more than 75,000 people and transforming community health.

Nigeria, Ghana, Mali 75,000+ Beneficiaries

25 Mosques Constructed

Built 25 community mosques serving as spiritual centers and community gathering spaces, strengthening faith and social cohesion in underserved areas.

Northern Nigeria 50,000+ Worshippers

15,000+ Students Educated

Provided educational support including scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure improvements benefiting over 15,000 students across 85 schools.

85 Schools 15,000+ Students

Housing for Displaced Families

Constructed temporary and permanent housing for 500+ families displaced by conflicts and natural disasters, providing safe shelter and dignity.

Multiple Regions 500+ Families

Food Distribution Programs

Distributed over 2 million meals to vulnerable communities during Ramadan, emergencies, and ongoing food security programs across Africa.

Ongoing 2M+ Meals Served

Mobile Health Clinics

Operated mobile health clinics providing free medical care, vaccinations, and health education to over 30,000 people in remote areas with limited healthcare access.

Rural Communities 30,000+ Patients
