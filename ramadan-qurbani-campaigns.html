Ramadan & Qurbani Campaigns

Bringing communities together during the holy months of Ramadan and Eid through spiritual fulfillment, charitable giving, and community support programs.

Sacred Seasons of Giving

Our Ramadan and Qurbani campaigns honor the sacred traditions of giving and sharing, ensuring that the blessings of these holy months reach the most vulnerable members of our communities across Africa and beyond.

Ramadan Support

Iftar meals, Suhoor packages, and spiritual programs during the holy month

Our Campaign Programs

Comprehensive support during the holy months

Iftar Meals

Daily iftar meals for fasting communities, ensuring no one breaks their fast alone

  • Hot, nutritious meals
  • Community gathering spaces
  • Spiritual programs

  • Dates and traditional foods
  • Family-sized portions

Qurbani Meat Distribution

  • Halal certified meat
  • Proper distribution
  • Refrigerated storage

  • Festive activities
