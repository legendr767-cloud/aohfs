Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and how you can get involved

How can I volunteer with AOHF?

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds! You can apply through our volunteer page, attend our orientation sessions, or contact us directly. We have opportunities for both local and remote volunteering, including field work, administrative support, fundraising, and professional services.

Where does my donation go?

Your donations go directly to our humanitarian programs including education, healthcare, clean water projects, food distribution, and emergency relief. We maintain complete transparency with detailed reports on how funds are used, and you'll receive updates on the impact of your contribution.

In which countries does AOHF operate?

AOHF primarily operates across Africa, with programs in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and several other African countries. We also respond to international crises and work with partner organizations globally to extend our reach to vulnerable communities worldwide.

How can my organization partner with AOHF?

We welcome partnerships with corporations, NGOs, government agencies, and other organizations. Partnership opportunities include funding specific programs, providing expertise, joint initiatives, and corporate social responsibility programs. Contact us to discuss how we can work together.

Are donations to AOHF tax-deductible?

Yes, AOHF is a registered humanitarian organization, and donations are tax-deductible according to local tax laws. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes. Please consult with your tax advisor for specific guidance.
